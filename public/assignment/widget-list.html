Reds Motivated by Tough Tests

First up for the Reds in a busy month of matches is the visit of Stoke City on Sunday, followed by Liverpool away on Monday 17 October. A home game in the Europa League against Fenerbahce (and Robin van Persie) follows three days later, before Mourinho returns to his former club for a meeting with Chelsea on Sunday 23 October. Manchester City come to Old Trafford for the League Cup fourth-round tie the following Wednesday, before United host Burnley in the league on Saturday 29 October.
